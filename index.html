ITI

Industrial Training Institute

Industrial Training Institutes are government-backed vocational schools that provide hands-on practical training in technical and non-technical trades such as Electrician, COPA, and Draughtsman Civil. These courses help students become job-ready for different industries.

ATC

Advanced Technology Centre

Advanced Technology Centres are modern upgraded ITI training facilities introduced through government partnerships with major industry groups such as Tata Technologies. ATCs focus on high-tech, current industry-demand skills beyond older traditional courses.
